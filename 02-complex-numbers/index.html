Why Complex Numbers in Quantum Computing?

Complex numbers are fundamental to quantum mechanics and quantum computing. Quantum states are described using complex probability amplitudes, and quantum operations are represented by complex matrices. Understanding complex numbers is essential for working with qubits.

Why Complex Numbers Matter

Essential for Quantum Mechanics
